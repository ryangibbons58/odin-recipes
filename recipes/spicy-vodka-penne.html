Spicy Vodka Penne

Description

This penne alla vodka recipe is quick to make and a nice change from your typical tomato pasta dish. The sauce is rich and creamy with a little kick of heat from chili flakes.

Ingredients

  • 1lb uncooked pasta
  • 1 TBSP olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 small shallot
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1.5 oz vodka
  • 1 cup grated parmasan
  • 2 TBSP butter
  • Basil for garnishing

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil over medium heat in a saucepan
  2. Finely chop your garlic and shallot and add to the olive oil
  3. After about 3-4 minutes, add the tomato paste to the pan
  4. Cook for about 3-4 minutes, then add your vodka
  5. Stir until the vodka cooks off, then turn the heat down to low
  6. Stir in your heavy cream and red pepper flakes
  7. Once the cream is fully incorporated, add butter and parmasan to the sauce
  8. Cook your pasta until al dente
  9. Add cooked pasta directly to the sauce
  10. Add 1/2 of pasta water to the sauce and stir the pasta in
  11. Add fresh basil and more parmasan as a garnish
