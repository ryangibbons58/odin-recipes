Belgian Waffles

Description

This light and fluffy homemade Belgian Waffle recipe is so easy to make from scratch. Try adding fruit or other toppings and I think youâ€™ll find you will never need another waffle recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 3 TBPS Peanut Oil
  • 1 TSP Salt
  • 3 TBPS Sugar
  • 1 TSP Vanilla Extract
  • 1 TSP Baking Powder
  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 2 Cups Milk

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, oil, salt, sugar and extract
  2. Sift in the flour and baking powder
  3. Stir in milk until a thick batter comes together
  4. Pour batter into pre-heated waffle maker
  5. Remove from waffle maker when golden brown and cooked throughout
  6. Top with butter and syrup
