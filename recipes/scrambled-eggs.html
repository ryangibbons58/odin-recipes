Scrambled Eggs

Description

In search of tender, fluffy scrambled eggs? Here are the only ingredients you need, and the chef-approved method for how to make scrambled eggs worthy of any morning. And, no... you don't add milk!

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 TBSP Butter
  • salt to taste
  • green onion

Steps

  1. Melt butter over medium-low heat in a non-stick pan
  2. Crack eggs into bowl, and once butter is melted, add to the pan
  3. Gently stir the eggs with a spatula, breaking the yolks
  4. Continually stir the eggs and monitor the heat; you don't want the eggs to set too quickly
  5. Remove pan from the heat as necessary to slow cook the eggs
  6. Eggs should slowly start to set after about 7-8 minutes
  7. Once cooked to your liking, add salt to taste
  8. Plate the eggs and add a garnish of chopped green onion
